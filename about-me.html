Biography

Wenke Huang completed his Master of Science Degree in Statistics at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in December 2017. While being in the graduate school, he was also working as a Graduate Assistant for the School of Information Science of the Univ. of IL. From there, he learned more knowledge of Data Science and Statistics and enhanced his practical computer programming experience.

He is specialized in Data Science, Statistics, Machine Learning and Software Engineering. He enjoys drowning in data and discovering stories behind it. He has worked on multiple statistical consulting projects where he helped his clients solve problems by performing professional statistical analysis and building predictive models. His clients are from different departments of the Univ. of IL including Department of Statistics and Krannert Center for the Performing Arts.

Before pursuing his master's degree in the United States, he was an associate at China Merchants Bank in Shanghai, China. He obtained his Bachelor's Degree in Statistics from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics in July 2014.

In his spare time, he loves playing table tennis with his friends. He is also a member of both Chinese Karatedo Association (CKA) and Japan Karate Shoto Federation (JKS).
