Hi! I'm Wenke Huang, a Statistician at the Center for Health Policy Research of the University of California, Los Angeles. I joined the Center in March 2018 after obtaining my MSc in Statistics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in December 2017.

My current responsiblities include preparing California Health Interview Survey (CHIS) data, maintaining Center's internal R packages and website applications, and offering statistical support for resarchers. I myself am a big fan of open sources R(cpp), Python and JavaScript while at the same time holding four SAS certifications.

I am working or have worked in the following areas:

  • Data Management, Analysis and Mining
  • Statistical Tests and Forecasting
  • Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Pattern Recognition
  • Website Development and Software Engineering

Outside of work and study, I enjoy playing ping-pong with my friends. I was also a member of the Japan Karate Shoto Federation until my retirement in 2015.
