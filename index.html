About

Hi! I'm Wenke Huang, a Statistician of the Center for Health Policy Research at the University of California, Los Angeles. I joined the Center in Mar. 2018 after obtaining my MSc in Statistics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Dec. 2017.

My current responsiblities include preparing California Health Interview Survey (CHIS) data, maintaining Center's internal R packages and web apps, and providing statistical support to resarchers. I'm also developing an R package surveyfreq with C++ concentrating on survey analysis now. I myself am a big fan of open sources R, Python and JavaScript while at the same time holding four SAS certifications.

Outside of work and study, I enjoy playing ping-pong with my friends. I was also a member of Chinese Karatedo Association and Japan Karate Shoto Federation and retired in 2015.

Code

Most of my works are in the form of R(cpp), SAS, Python and JavaScript. Because of the confidentiality agreement and the sensitity of CHIS data, I'm not allowed to share my codes for UCLA with the public without having the Center's prior permission.
