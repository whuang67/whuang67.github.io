About

Hi! I'm Wenke Huang, a Statistician at the Center for Health Policy Research of the University of California, Los Angeles. I joined the Center in Mar. 2018 after obtaining my MSc in Statistics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Dec. 2017.

My current responsiblities include preparing California Health Interview Survey (CHIS) data, maintaining Center's internal R packages and web applications, and providing statistical support to resarchers. I'm also developing an R package surveyfreq with C++ concentrating on survey analysis. I myself am a big fan of open sources R, Python and JavaScript while at the same time holding four SAS certifications.

Outside of work and study, I enjoy playing ping-pong with my friends. I was also a member of Chinese Karatedo Association and Japan Karate Shoto Federation and retired in 2015.

Code
